Today's Cosmic Weather

As Mars crosses the threshold from steady Taurus into mercurial Gemini, the cosmic atmosphere shifts from slow and deliberate to fast and multi-directional. This transit will remain in effect until mid-May, gradually redirecting collective energy away from material security and toward intellectual exploration. Today marks just the beginning of this lively six-week cycle.

Key themes: Communication, curiosity, short journeys, new contacts, ideas, multitasking.
